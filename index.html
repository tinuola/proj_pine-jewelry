Every Pine Jewelry piece is created with great attention to detail using a combination of both new items, antiques, and recycled materials. This means every piece is unique.

We have been hand crafting jewelry since 1993

Our location in historic Flagstaff leaves us with access to some of the best natural materials and antiques in the country. We maintain sustainable practices and always think about the environment first.

Custom pieces are available upon request

If you're looking for a custom item or having an heirloom used to create a new piece, we will work with you to make sure your vision becomes a reality.

Our inspiration comes both from nature, and being in the historic Wild West.
